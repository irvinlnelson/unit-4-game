Crystal Collector

Don't let Walter White getaway!

How To Play

Help Hank and the DEA stop the infamous Walter White.

Hank will give you the number of crystals to collect at the beginning of each game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You will win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the number of crystals given to you at the beginning of the game.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
